GoFriends development team grew up to 20 people. The main directions for software development are Ruby on Rails, PHP, Laravel, Yii/YII2, Javascript and for mobile development – Swift, Objective C, Java, Android SDK.

The company has already completed 40 projects and began to develop projects in the field of cryptocurrency and Multi-level Marketing Systems.

There are constantly held English Speaking Courses in all departments in order to understand better our client. Also every day we do sports lessons together. Team actively participates in international exhibitions, conferences, and trainings, constantly improving professional and communicative skills and increasing qualification byattending IT courses.

Furthermore, we participated in Hackathon and Demo Day for Ukrainian start-ups as mentors for start-upers.
